### Our Vision

 Our vision serves as the framework for our Roadmap and guides every aspect of our business by describing what we need to accomplish in order to continue achieving sustainable, quality growth
*   People: Be a great place to work where people are inspired to be the best they can be
*   Partners: Nurture a winning network of customers and suppliers, together we create mutual, enduring value.
*   Profit: Maximize long-term return to Our Organization and to the Employees while being mindful of our overall responsibilities
*   Productivity: Be an highly effective, lean and fast-moving organization
    
    ### Our Mission
    
     Our Mission is Caring for the Patient, one person at a time.... We embrace bringing innovative ideas and services to advance the health and well-being of people. Employees of Sanshok work with partners in health care to touch the lives of people every day, throughout.
